02. Define

Problem Definition & Analysis

Synthesizing research findings into clear problem statements, competitive analysis, and user experience mapping to guide solution development.

Core Problem Statement

Designers struggle with inconsistent feedback quality rather than feedback speed, leading to extended revision cycles and decreased confidence in design decisions. Current tools optimize for workflow collaboration but lack intelligence in feedback quality assessment.

Analysis

Current Tool Limitations

Existing tools like Figma, InVision, and Maze focus on workflow optimization and comment collection but provide no intelligence about feedback quality or actionability.

Market Gap

Validation Intelligence

No current solutions analyze feedback quality, provide design validation scoring, or offer objective design assessment capabilities.

Opportunity

AI Integration Potential

Opportunity exists for intelligent layer between designers and feedback providers, focusing on quality enhancement rather than workflow speed.
